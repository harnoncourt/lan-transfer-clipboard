# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming and useful place for contributors and users.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about scope and priorities.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, spam, and publishing private information without permission.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming, please contact the maintainers through a private GitHub security advisory or another private channel available on the repository.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other action needed to protect the project.
